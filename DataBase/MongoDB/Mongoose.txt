- Mongoose allows you to define a schema for your data, providing a clear structure and validation for your documents.
- Mongoose provides an easy-to-use API for performing queries on MongoDB, making retrieving,updating,and deleting documents more straightforward.
- Mongoose offers middleware hooks to execute functions before or after specific DB operations, enabling custom logic and data manipulation.

- Models are fancy constructors compiled from Schema definations.
- An instance of a model is called a document
